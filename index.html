Heavenly Bodies and Celestial Objects

Space is filled with fascinating objects, each telling a story of the universe's vastness. Stars, like our Sun, are giant nuclear reactors powering entire solar systems. Planets orbit stars, with some, like Earth, supporting life. Moons accompany planets, shaping tides and landscapes. Asteroids and comets are ancient space debris, remnants from the solar system's formation.
