Our Solar System

The solar system is a vast cosmic neighborhood centered around the Sun, a massive star that provides light and energy. It consists of eight planets — Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune — each orbiting the Sun in unique paths. Alongside planets, there are moons, asteroids, comets, and dwarf planets like Pluto. The solar system showcases diverse worlds, from scorching Venus to icy Neptune, highlighting the wonders of space.
